24/7 Teach
Project Overview
Role: UX/UI & Graphic Design
Time: 3 Months
Platform: Desktop
For my virtual internship at 24/7 Teach, I redesigned the company's Learning Academy and Learning Center websites. I used Squarespace to implement my designs from conception and also created custom graphic design images to improve product branding.
Process
I did a lot of competitor analysis to research the most important elements needed for a virtual classroom website and the best way to organize them. Afterwards, I created wireframes of each landing page and went through multiple design iterations with the CEO until final approval.
Designs & Prototypes
I created higher-fidelity mockups and used Squarespace to implement my final designs. I created graphic design images to enhance the engageability of all the educational content including banners, customer stories, infographics, icons, and cover photos.
24/7 Teach Learning Academy
The Learning Academy is both a physical private school and an online virtual classroom for students aged 11-19. I designed the flow and interface of all their landing pages and created images to promote their educational content.
24/7 Teach Learning Center
I also redesigned their Learning Center, an online project-based curriculum for students aged 11-19, as the backend was being developed. Features included the Course Overview, Grid & List View of Courses, different states of Instructor Adding Courses, Instructor Message Center, Instructor Dashboard, and Instructor Course View.
