CBS Interactive - Games Group
Project Overview
Role: UX/UI Design
Time: 3 Months
Platform: Desktop, Mobile
At CBS Interactive, I worked with the Games Group which manages GameSpot, Giant Bomb, and Comic Vine. My time was spent on redesigning GameSpot's website. GameSpot went through a redesign 3 years ago, but the profile page had been neglected for a long time. My task was to redesign the profile page on GameSpot to improve the user experience, flow, and engageability. Unfortunately, I can not show any of my designs here but I can talk about my processes behind them.
Process
With such a large and open-ended project, a lot of my time was spent on higher organizational thinking in order to find the right direction to take the profile. My design processes included:
Researching competitors
Studying user analytics
Grouping features into buckets to seek patterns
Creating affinity diagrams
Brainstorming actionable solutions
Designs & Prototypes
I mockuped the first iteration of the profile and made clickable prototypes. For user testing, we interviewed a few interns & four GameSpot moderators. I also proposed a new 'Games I Follow' feature that became the most positively recieved feature.
Solution
I remade the mockups with the feedback we gained from user testing and the internal product design team. The final redesign was approved, and the prototypes were implemented for QA Testing. I presented my research and final redesigns at the Intern Showcase.
